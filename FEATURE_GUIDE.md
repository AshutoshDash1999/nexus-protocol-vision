## 🔒 3. PRIVACY NEGOTIATOR - Cryptographic Communication

### What It Is
An **autonomous agent that negotiates with external systems** on your behalf using advanced cryptography to achieve goals WITHOUT exposing your personal data.

### The Problem It Solves
- **Today**: You share all your data with hospitals, banks, governments
- **Privacy Negotiator**: Proves you're trustworthy WITHOUT revealing the data

### The Cryptographic Arsenal

#### Multi-Party Computation (MPC)
Multiple parties compute together without revealing individual data.
```
Hospital A: {encrypted_patient_data}
Hospital B: {encrypted_patient_data}
→ System computes disease patterns (no one sees raw data)
```

#### Zero-Knowledge Proofs (ZKP)
Prove something WITHOUT revealing the proven information.
```
✓ "I'm over 18" (verified without showing ID)
✓ "My credit score > 700" (verified without sharing it)
✓ "I have $10,000 in savings" (verified without showing account)
```

#### Secure Aggregation
Combine data from multiple sources into statistics WITHOUT revealing individual data.

### The Negotiation Process (5 Steps)
1. **Request Received** → External system needs information from you
2. **Privacy Boundary Check** → Can we use cryptography instead of raw data?
3. **Cryptographic Protocol Selection** → Choose MPC, ZKP, or Secure Aggregation
4. **Secure Computation** → Data encrypted, processed securely
5. **Result Delivered** → External system gets what they need, your privacy intact

### Real-World Examples
- **Healthcare**: Prove you're not on disqualifying medications WITHOUT revealing diagnoses
- **Loans**: Prove creditworthiness WITHOUT sharing salary or account balances
- **Smart Cities**: Contribute traffic data WITHOUT revealing your home/workplace

### Privacy Budget
- Each negotiation "costs" some of your privacy budget
- Set monthly limits
- System prioritizes cryptographic solutions (low cost) over data sharing (high cost)

### How to Use
1. Navigate to **"Privacy Negotiator"** page
2. View the **Live Negotiations Monitor** showing active negotiations
3. See status icons for pending/negotiating/approved/rejected requests
4. Check privacy guarantees being applied for each negotiation

## 🌱 4. CARBON-AWARE OPTIMIZER - Sustainable Computing

### What It Is
Monitors and optimizes **environmental impact** of computation in real-time.

### Why It Matters
- Training one AI model = 125 transatlantic flights in CO₂
- Most AI systems ignore environmental cost entirely
- Carbon-Aware Optimizer makes smarter decisions about WHEN and HOW to compute

### Core Metrics
1. **Energy Usage (kWh)** → Raw energy consumed
2. **Carbon Emission (kg CO₂)** → Impact based on your grid mix
3. **Efficiency Score (0-100%)** → How efficiently computation ran
4. **Temporal Awareness** → Knows when renewables are abundant

### How It Optimizes
1. **Model Pruning** → Use simple model for routine tasks, complex model only when needed
2. **Batch Processing** → Group similar computations together
3. **Task Scheduling** → Defer non-urgent tasks to renewable energy windows
4. **Caching & Reuse** → Reuse cached results instead of recomputing
5. **Graceful Degradation** → Offer fast/lower-resolution answer if carbon is high

### You're in Control
- **Set Carbon Budget** → Define monthly/daily CO₂ limit
- **Quality vs Carbon Trade-off** → Choose between Eco, Balanced, or Quality modes

### Real Impact
- Without Carbon Awareness: 50 kg CO₂/month (1600 kg/year)
- With Carbon Awareness: 15 kg CO₂/month (180 kg/year)
- **Savings: 1,420 kg CO₂/year** = 3 months without a car!

### How to Use
1. Go to **"Carbon Aware"** page
2. View the **Carbon Impact Dashboard** showing real-time metrics
3. See energy usage, carbon emissions, efficiency scores
4. Review optimization opportunities and potential savings
